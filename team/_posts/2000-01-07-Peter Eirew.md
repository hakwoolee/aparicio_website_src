Dr Eirew has a varied academic and work background, with degrees in Physics (Cambridge University, UK), MSc Molecular Medicine (Imperial College London), Masters in Business Administration (Manchester Business School), and PhD research identifying stem cells in the human breast (University of British Columbia, supervisor Dr Connie Eaves).
